Shop My Luxury Brand, a.k.a. SMLB, is the Malaysia largest pre-owned luxury goods platform. SMLB is a marketplace for unused luxury items like Louis Vuitton, Hermes, Chanel, Gucci, etc. Buying and selling on SMLB App is surprisingly easy and hassle-free with a user-friendly interface, smooth listing procedure and a responsive, friendly customer service team.
